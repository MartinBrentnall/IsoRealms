Sanity checks for hidden surface removal algorithms
---------------------------------------------------

In order to carry out sanity checks, the following procedure should be followed:

1.  Choose ONE of the desired sanity check.  Choices are: floor, wall, ceiling.
2.  Disable rendering of those surfaces NOT chosen.
3.  Load the sanity check map for the chosen surface.
4.  The map contains several pairs of surfaces.  Observe that the pairs are
    identical.  If the pairs are not identical, a problem is present.
